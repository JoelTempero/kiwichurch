Our Heart

Why We Give

Generosity is at the heart of following Jesus. We give because we've received so much. We give because it's a tangible way to participate in God's work. We give because it shapes our hearts and reminds us that our resources belong to God.

As a small community of house churches, we keep our costs low. Your giving supports:

  • Shared meals and hospitality
  • Resources and materials for our gatherings
  • Community events and outreach
  • Supporting those in need within our community
  • Contributing to wider church and mission work

We believe in radical generosity - not just giving to the church, but giving to each other and to those in need around us.

Generosity

Other Ways to Give

Give Your Time

Volunteer with one of our communities - help with hospitality, music, kids, or practical needs.

Give Your Skills

Offer your professional skills to serve the community - whether that's tech help, counselling, design, or something else.

Give Practically

Contribute food for shared meals, offer your home as a gathering space, or help someone in need.

Give Beyond Us

We encourage everyone to practice generosity beyond Kiwi Church - support local charities, help neighbours, and give to those in need in our city.

Questions?

If you have questions about giving, financial accountability, or how we use funds, please get in touch. We're committed to transparency.
